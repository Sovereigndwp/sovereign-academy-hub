About The Academy

About The Sovereign Academy.

The Sovereign Academy is a financial education and training company created to help people make stronger decisions about money, risk, and personal responsibility.

We build more than lessons. We create complete learning systems that can be used directly by individuals or delivered through organizations that already serve them.

How We Work

Principles that shape every program.

Practice before theory

Learners work through real decisions — spending, borrowing, saving, protecting — instead of abstract lectures.

Clear language

Plain explanations without talking down to learners. Jargon is defined or removed.

Materials adapted to the setting

A classroom, a job site, a reentry program, and a kitchen table are different rooms. The materials respect that.

Instructor support, not just participant content

Facilitator guides, delivery notes, and preparation come with the curriculum, not as an afterthought.

Privacy-respecting tools

Learning tools that don't harvest personal data or require accounts to start learning.

Education, not sales

Financial education without selling financial products — and a careful distinction between education and individualized financial, legal, or tax advice.

Train the Trainer

Strong curriculum is not enough if instructors are unprepared to deliver it.

The Sovereign Academy creates facilitator guides, instructor preparation, delivery notes, scenario prompts, discussion questions, and implementation standards. This helps partner organizations teach the material with consistency while still adapting it to the people in the room.

Outreach and Specialized Populations

Designed for the actual conditions in which people learn.

Many financial education programs assume stable internet access, conventional employment, strong reading skills, access to mainstream banking, and freedom to act immediately. Those assumptions do not fit every learner.

The Sovereign Academy designs programs for the actual conditions in which people learn. This may include paper-first delivery, limited internet access, bilingual instruction, low-risk practice, reentry constraints, variable income, unfamiliarity with financial institutions, or the needs of first-time facilitators.

Founder

Built and run by an educator.

Dalia Platt

The Sovereign Academy was founded by Dalia Platt, an educator and curriculum developer who has spent six years creating practical financial and Bitcoin education in English and Spanish.

Her work has included public education, structured curricula, interactive learning tools, facilitator resources, institutional programs, and training designed for communities often overlooked by conventional financial education.
