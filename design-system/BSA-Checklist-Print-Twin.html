◆Key ceremony

Self-custody setup, verified at every step

■ Stop — read first

Your seed words never touch a keyboard, a camera, a photo, a cloud note, or a password manager. Handwriting on the supplied card is the only copy that exists. Anyone who sees the words controls the money.

PHASE 1 Prepare
Buy the signer directly from the manufacturer
never second-hand · never from a marketplace reseller
Inspect the tamper seal before opening
if anything looks disturbed: stop, photograph, contact manufacturer
VERIFY
Check the firmware signature yourself
compare the fingerprint on the device screen with the one published at the manufacturer's site — type the URL by hand
PHASE 2 Generate & record
Generate the seed on the device — never import one
a seed that ever existed anywhere else is not yours alone
Write all 24 words, in order, in pen
use the numbered card supplied with module 07
VERIFY
Read the card back against the device, word by word
out loud, finger on each word — most recovery failures are transcription errors made here
PHASE 3 Prove recovery before funding
Wipe the device and restore it from your handwritten card
yes, really — before a single sat arrives
Send a small test amount, then send it back out
receiving proves the address; spending proves the keys
VERIFY
Confirm the receive address on the device screen, not the computer
first 4 + last 4 characters, every time, forever
