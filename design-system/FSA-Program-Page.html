◆How it works

Scope, pilot, then scale.

STEP 1 · SCOPE

A 30-minute call about your setting, constraints, and cohort size. You get a recommended module sequence and a sample kit to review.

STEP 2 · PILOT

One cohort, full materials, our support. We revise the scenarios with your facilitators' notes before anything scales.

STEP 3 · SCALE

Your program reproduces materials under license and runs independently. We stay available for facilitator questions and content updates.
