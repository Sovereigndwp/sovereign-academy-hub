Make a budget you can keep

The scenario

Marcus starts a new job next week. He will bring home $1,480 every two weeks. His rent is $650 a month, his phone is $45, and he owes his cousin $200. He wants to stop running out of money before payday.

3. Your turn: what is one bill you would set aside first, and why?
Words to know

Budget — a plan for money before it arrives.

Must-pay — a bill with a real consequence if missed.

Remember

$1,480 every two weeks is about $2,960 a month — but count the month with only two paydays.

Facilitator cue

Give 6 minutes for step 1. Answers vary — the total matters less than naming every must-pay. Watch for anyone stuck on the two-week vs monthly math.
