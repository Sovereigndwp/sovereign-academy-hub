How we build

Few, finished, high-craft.

A small family run well beats a sprawl of half-built sites. These are the rules that keep it honest.

Earn the home

Content goes live only with a named audience, a way to sustain it, and a commitment to maintain it. Until then it incubates.

Bitcoin, not noise

We teach Bitcoin and real financial skills — not trading calls, maximalist discourse, or altcoin speculation.

Practice over lectures

Interactive simulations and 20-minute modules. You learn by doing it safely, before it counts.

Your data stays yours

No wallet required to learn, open-source tools, and no harvesting of personal data. Sovereignty starts with us too.
