What the Academy Does

Education is only useful when people can apply it.

The Sovereign Academy develops financial education that people can use in real life. Our work ranges from free public learning tools to structured programs for workforce organizations, schools, correctional and reentry settings, employers, municipalities, and community partners.

We also prepare facilitators and partner organizations to deliver the material with clarity, consistency, and respect for the people they serve.

Practical Curriculum

Clear lessons, exercises, scenarios, and tools built around financial decisions people face in real life.

Institutional Programs

Structured programs that can be used by employers, workforce organizations, schools, correctional and reentry programs, municipalities, and community partners.

Train the Trainer

Facilitator guides, preparation materials, delivery standards, and training that help organizations teach the content consistently.

Bilingual and Population-Aware Design

English and Spanish materials designed with the needs, constraints, and lived experiences of different communities in mind.

For Organizations

Bring practical financial education to the people you serve.

The Sovereign Academy works with organizations that want to add meaningful financial education to an existing training, workforce, education, or community program.

Our programs can support employees, skilled workers, students, justice-impacted adults, families, educators, and community members. Materials can include participant lessons, facilitator guides, assessments, real-life scenarios, implementation support, and train-the-trainer preparation — with delivery available in English and Spanish.
